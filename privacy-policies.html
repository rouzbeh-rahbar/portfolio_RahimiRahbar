These are small text files that enable us to recognize your browser on your next visit. Google Analytics uses "cookies" to enable us to analyze the use of our website and thus to further develop our offer and our website (= legitimate interest) in line with requirements. The information generated by the cookie is usually transmitted to a Google server in the USA, together with the IP code, and stored there. We only use Google Analytics with activated IP anonymization. This means that your IP address will be shortened beforehand by Google within member states of the European Union or in other contracting states of the Agreement on the European Economic Area. Only in exceptional cases will the full IP address be sent to a Google server in the USA and only pseudonymised there by shortening it. You have the option of preventing the storage of cookies on your end device at any time by making the appropriate settings in your browser, which, however, may limit the range of functions of our offer. You can also prevent Google from collecting the data generated by the cookie and related to your website usage and from processing this data by Google by downloading and installing the browser plug-in available under the following link: https://tools. google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=en
